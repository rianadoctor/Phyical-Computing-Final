# Milestone 2 — First Pattern Formation

## Goal
The goal of this stage was to improve system stability and determine whether consistent vibration patterns could emerge under controlled conditions.

---

## Improvements Made
- Strengthened mounting of steel plate  
- Improved alignment of speaker-to-plate connection  
- Adjusted salt distribution  
- Tested a wider range of frequencies  

---

## Observations
- First visible nodal patterns began to appear  
- Certain frequencies consistently produced structured shapes  
- Other frequencies produced no response or chaotic motion  
- Pattern clarity depended heavily on physical setup stability  

---

## Key Insight: Resonance Emerges Here
This stage made it clear that **resonance is not continuous—it is selective**.

Only specific frequencies caused the system to “lock in” and produce structure. This showed that:

> The system is not reacting to sound in general—it is responding only when conditions align with its natural frequencies.

---

## Outcome
This milestone marked the transition from a non-functional prototype to a system capable of producing controlled physical patterns.
